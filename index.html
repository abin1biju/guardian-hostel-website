Details

Our hostel is the Thrissur's number 1 Girls Hostel.We provide rooms for college student or for a group of students in an institute or for working women.All rooms are for a single person.These three groups are placed in three different floors.As there is a facility of using lift it's easy to access to your rooms.The rents are calculated as per monthly basis or in an halfearly basis or in an annual basis.If you are taking rent only for a month, we will charge only Rs.5,000 and if you are taking rent in an halfearly basis, we will charge only Rs.27,500 and if you are taking rent in an annual basis, we will charge only Rs.50,000. Breakfast is included with the rent.
